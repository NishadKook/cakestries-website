I What We Offer

Three ways to learn.

01

PAMA Workshops

Public cake decorating sessions hosted at the Peel Art Gallery, Museum and Archives. Open to all skill levels. Learn buttercream techniques, piping, and fondant work — then take home your gourmet cupcakes.

Public · All Levels
02

Cake & Worship

A one-of-a-kind community experience created by Chantale — combining cake decorating with Christian worship. Come for the cake, stay for the connection. Featured in PAMA's Black History Month celebrations.

Community · Faith
03

Private Classes

One-on-one or small group sessions in the studio. Perfect for birthdays, bridal parties, or anyone who wants dedicated time with Chantale to master a specific technique.

Private · Custom
