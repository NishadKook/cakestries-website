The Studio

A small atelier with an unhurried hand.

Cakestries is a studio practice built on a simple belief: that a cake can be as considered as anything else at the table — and that the best ones are designed, not ordered.

I — Our Story

It started with one impossible request.

A friend wanted a cake that looked like a folded silk gown. Everyone said it couldn't be done in sugar. We spent a week proving otherwise — and somewhere in those late nights, Cakestries was born.

What began as a fascination with the limits of the medium became a discipline. We learned that the magic was never only in how a cake looked, but in the moment it was cut — when something beautiful turned out to taste like the best thing in the room.

Years on, the philosophy hasn't changed. We take a handful of commissions each week, design each from a blank page, and finish every one by hand. Slow, deliberate, and never twice the same.

II What We Hold To

The principles behind every cake.

01

Designed, not duplicated

No catalogue, no copy-paste. Every commission is drawn for one occasion and retired after it.

02

Flavour first

Beauty is the invitation; taste is the promise. We develop both in the same breath, from scratch.

03

Made by hand

Sugar petals, gilding, sculpted form — finished by one pair of hands, the slow way, every time.

04

Honest ingredients

Real butter, real fruit, seasonal where we can. Nothing on the plate we wouldn't serve our own table.

05

Few, done fully

We cap our week on purpose. Fewer cakes, more attention — the only way to keep the standard.

06

Your story, centred

The brief is always you — your palette, your people, the feeling you want left in the room.

III — The Hand Behind It

Part pâtissier,
part sculptor.

Cakestries is led by a maker trained in pastry and obsessed with form — someone equally at home piping a delicate sugar bloom or engineering a tier that defies gravity.

That dual instinct is the studio's signature: cakes built like architecture and finished like couture, yet rooted in proper, generous baking.
