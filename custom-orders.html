Commission a Cake

Made for one occasion only.

A Cakestries commission is a collaboration. You bring the occasion and the feeling; we bring the craft. Here's how it works, what's included, and how to begin.

I The Process

From first hello to final reveal.

i

Enquiry

Share your date, guest count and the vision. We reply within two working days to confirm availability.

ii

Design & Tasting

A concept sketch and a tasting box, so you approve both the look and the flavour before we build.

iii

The Making

Baked and hand-finished across several days in our studio — entirely from scratch, never frozen.

iv

Delivery & Styling

Hand-delivered and set in place across Toronto and the GTA, so it arrives flawless and ready.

II What Every Commission Includes

Everything your commission includes.

01

A bespoke design

An original concept drawn for your occasion alone, refined with you until it's right.

02

A private tasting

A curated box of our seasonal flavours so you choose with your palate, not a menu.

03

Hand finishing

Sugar work, gilding and detail completed by hand — the slow, careful part we never skip.

04

Safe delivery

Temperature-considered transport and on-site setup across Toronto, GTA and Peel Region.

05

Dietary care

Thoughtful options for many dietary needs, developed without compromising on taste.

06

One point of contact

The maker who designs your cake is the one who builds it — and the one you speak to throughout.
