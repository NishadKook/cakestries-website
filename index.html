I — Philosophy

We don't bake cakes.
We compose them.

One commission at a time, never twice the same. Cakestries began as a quiet obsession with detail — the fall of a ribbon of buttercream, the exact blush of a sugar petal.

Today it is a studio practice — part pâtisserie, part sculpture — where flavour and form are designed together, by hand.

IV — The Maker

A small atelier. An unhurried hand.

Every Cakestries commission is made by one pair of hands, from a studio that takes only a handful of orders each week. We would rather make less and make it exceptional.

That means real conversations, real tastings, and a design developed for you alone — never pulled from a catalogue.

— Cakestries Studio
