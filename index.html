I — Philosophy

We don't bake cakes.
We compose them.

Cakestries began when Chantale Abbott-Keith brought her eye for art, fashion, and architecture into the kitchen. Trained in pastry, she turned a creative obsession into a bespoke cake studio.

Today it is a studio practice — part pâtisserie, part sculpture — where flavour and form are designed together, by hand. Every commission is one of a kind.

IV — The Maker

A small studio. An unhurried hand.

Every Cakestries commission is made by Chantale, from a studio that takes only a handful of orders each week. We would rather make less and make it exceptional.

That means real conversations, real tastings, and a design developed for you alone — never pulled from a catalogue.

— Chantale Abbott-Keith
