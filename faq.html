Good to Know

Questions, answered.

Everything you might want to know before commissioning. If your question isn't here, the studio is always happy to talk it through.

For weddings and larger commissions, three to six months is ideal — we take a limited number of dates and they fill early, particularly in summer. For celebration cakes, two to four weeks is usually enough. That said, always ask: we occasionally keep room for last-minute pieces.
Celebration cakes begin at £180 and wedding commissions from £450. Because each cake is designed and made from scratch, this reflects the time and craft involved. We'll always give you a clear, tailored quote before anything is confirmed.
Yes — and we encourage it. Every wedding commission includes a curated tasting box of our seasonal flavours. For celebration cakes, tastings can be arranged on request. We'd rather you fall in love with the flavour before we build a thing.
We develop thoughtful options for many dietary needs — including vegetarian, egg-free and certain allergen-conscious recipes — without treating them as an afterthought. Our studio does handle nuts, gluten and dairy, so we cannot guarantee a fully allergen-free environment. Tell us your needs early and we'll be honest about what we can do beautifully.
Yes. Tiered and sculptural cakes are hand-delivered and set in place by us — it's the only way to guarantee they arrive as intended. Delivery within our local radius is included in most wedding quotes; further afield is arranged at cost. Smaller cakes can be collected from the studio by appointment.
A signed design proposal and a 25% deposit confirm your booking and reserve the date in our diary. The balance is due two weeks before your event. Until the deposit is received, dates remain open to other enquiries.
We love references as a starting point, but we won't copy another maker's work line-for-line — it isn't fair to them or true to us. Instead, bring us the images that move you and we'll design something original that captures the same feeling, made unmistakably yours.
Every commission arrives with simple care notes. As a rule: keep it cool and out of direct sun, bring it to room temperature an hour before serving for the best flavour and texture, and we'll show you exactly where to make the first cut on delivery.
Life happens. Design tweaks are welcome up to two weeks before your date. Deposits are non-refundable as they reserve time we turn others away for, but we'll always do our best to be fair and flexible where we can.
